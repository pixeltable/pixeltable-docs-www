# v0.4.3

**Released:** July 10, 2025
**Author:** [@aaron-siegel](https://github.com/aaron-siegel)
**View on GitHub:** [v0.4.3](https://github.com/pixeltable/pixeltable/releases/tag/v0.4.3)

---

## What's Changed
* Allow config parameters to be specified in `pxt.init()` by @aaron-siegel in https://github.com/pixeltable/pixeltable/pull/680
* Prepare to report more status in table.history() by @jpeterson-pxt in https://github.com/pixeltable/pixeltable/pull/682
* `pxt.ls()` command for pretty-printing all contents of a Pixeltable dir by @aaron-siegel in https://github.com/pixeltable/pixeltable/pull/681
* Handle 429 errors in RateLimitScheduler by @mkornacker in https://github.com/pixeltable/pixeltable/pull/670
* Support dicts and Sequences of dicts in HF datasets [rough-edges PXT-640] by @aaron-siegel in https://github.com/pixeltable/pixeltable/pull/684
* Allow packaging of non-snapshot tables in TablePackager by @aaron-siegel in https://github.com/pixeltable/pixeltable/pull/688
* Use a JSON field xxx_cellmd in place of xxx_errortype and xxx_errormsg by @jpeterson-pxt in https://github.com/pixeltable/pixeltable/pull/685
* Consolidate media operations in the MediaStore module by @jpeterson-pxt in https://github.com/pixeltable/pixeltable/pull/691
* Enhance UpdateStatus to subsume SyncStatus. Save user and UpdateStatus in a field in TableVersionMd. by @jpeterson-pxt in https://github.com/pixeltable/pixeltable/pull/689
* Refactor create_replica to conform to concurrency protocol by @aaron-siegel in https://github.com/pixeltable/pixeltable/pull/690
* Add additional packages & task configurations to nightly.yml by @aaron-siegel in https://github.com/pixeltable/pixeltable/pull/693
* Doc fixes for audio and video UDFs by @aaron-siegel in https://github.com/pixeltable/pixeltable/pull/698


**Full Changelog**: https://github.com/pixeltable/pixeltable/compare/v0.4.2...v0.4.3
