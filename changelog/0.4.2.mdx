## What's Changed
* Revert various accumulated workarounds in CI by @aaron-siegel in https://github.com/pixeltable/pixeltable/pull/669
* Use ColumnHandles in external stores by @aaron-siegel in https://github.com/pixeltable/pixeltable/pull/664
* Update versions of a few more libraries by @aaron-siegel in https://github.com/pixeltable/pixeltable/pull/668
* First part of additional status collection for table.history reporting. by @jpeterson-pxt in https://github.com/pixeltable/pixeltable/pull/654
* Add table.history() method to return a user-readable list of known changes to a table.  by @jpeterson-pxt in https://github.com/pixeltable/pixeltable/pull/640
* Added Table.recompute_columns() by @mkornacker in https://github.com/pixeltable/pixeltable/pull/667
* Collect more information on ins, del, upd operations. Freeze UpdateStatus. by @jpeterson-pxt in https://github.com/pixeltable/pixeltable/pull/673
* Refactor SyncStatus for merge with UpdateStatus. by @jpeterson-pxt in https://github.com/pixeltable/pixeltable/pull/674
* Adding recompute_columns() to overview in table.md by @mkornacker in https://github.com/pixeltable/pixeltable/pull/675
* CI workflow for random table ops by @aaron-siegel in https://github.com/pixeltable/pixeltable/pull/676
* ~40% improvement in insert performance by @aaron-siegel in https://github.com/pixeltable/pixeltable/pull/658
* Skip whisperx on t4 instances by @aaron-siegel in https://github.com/pixeltable/pixeltable/pull/678
* Pretty-print update status in notebooks or IPython shells by @aaron-siegel in https://github.com/pixeltable/pixeltable/pull/677
* Performance improvements in add_computed_column by @aaron-siegel in https://github.com/pixeltable/pixeltable/pull/679
